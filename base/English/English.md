- 我很无聊

- 你从哪里来?

where do you come from?

where are you from?

- 我怕热

I don't like the heat.

- 我是中国人

I am Chinese.

- 雨很大

The rain is heavy.

- 我很喜欢你

I like you very much.

- 今天有课

I have class today.

There is class today.

- 我会听音乐

I will listen to music.

- 开灯

Turn on the light.

Turn on the stove.

Here you are.

I will go to Thailand for a visit.

I have been to Canada.

No reason.

- 那边有一只狗

There is a dog there.

I'm English teacher.

I am home.

- 我在公园

- 运动很好玩
